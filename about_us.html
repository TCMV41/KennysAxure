Kenny's Sports Centre's Main Branch and Administration Office was incorporated in November 1985 and is the one of the largest Sporting Retail Companies in Trinidad and Tobago.

Kenny's Sports Centre offers a wide range of sporting and exercise equipment for almost every type of sport. While the majority of the sporting items are imported from England, the United States, Brazil, Canada and the Far East, Kenny's Sports also has its own line of Sportswear called "Sovereign" which is manufactured locally. Kenny's Sports has always operated on the advertising campaign of "the best possible price for a quality item". Much emphasis is placed on offering team uniforms to schools and customers at competitive prices. Kenny's Sports also has many customers in the Caribbean Islands including Barbados, Grenada, St. Vincent, St. Lucia, Dominica, St. Maarten, St. Kitts and Antigua. Kenny's Sports Centre Administration Office is situated at 87 Queen Street, Port of Spain, Trinidad.
